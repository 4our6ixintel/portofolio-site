How to Use Google AdSense

AdSense lets you earn money by placing ads on your website. Here’s how to get started:

  1. Create content: Your site must have useful, original pages (blog, projects, etc).
  2. Apply at adsense.google.com with your site’s URL.
  3. Wait for approval: Google reviews your site for quality and policy compliance.
  4. Place ad code: Once approved, you get a script to paste into your site.

Make sure your website has a privacy policy, contact page, and real value for visitors!
